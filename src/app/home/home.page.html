About Section

"A young, faithful, 'strapping' computer programmer whose coding history goes back to late 2017" isn't how I'd introduce myself. But hey? It does leave an impression, huh?


Like I mentioned ten seconds ago, my background stems from when I was a young lad in high school, entering my senior year. Wanting to prep for college, I searched for a way forward, unsure of what major to pursue. Then, after observing my older brother tinkering with a website for a class assignment, he showed me all the neat things he could do. So I gave it a try.


After I did, I've never looked back since.


I have experience in frontend programming, with a mild understanding of the backend. My main languages include Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and frameworks like Angular, Ionic, and Bootstrap. I love history, particularly American history. So if you need a patriotic website man to do the job, I'm your man. o7
